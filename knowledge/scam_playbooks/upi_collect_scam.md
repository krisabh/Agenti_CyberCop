# UPI Collect Request Scam Pattern

Scammers often pretend that your bank account will be blocked and ask you to approve a UPI collect request.

## Common signs

- urgent words: "immediately", "blocked", "suspend"
- asks for UPI ID and approval of collect request
- asks for OTP, PIN, password, or screen-share

## Safe probing ideas

- Ask for official helpline number.
- Ask branch name and employee ID.
- Ask them to share an official domain verification link.
